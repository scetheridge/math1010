    For problems 12-15, identify if a logical fallacy was used in the argument; explain.

  12. The Wyoming toad has not been seen outside of captivity since 2002, so it must be extinct in the wild.

    13. Fallacy (Appeal to Ignorance); the toad may be good at hiding.

  13. After Will said that we should put more money into health and education, Warren responded by saying that he was surprised that Will hates our country so much that he wants to leave it defenseless by cutting military spending.

    14. Fallacy (Straw Man); Will did not say he wanted to decrease military funding.

  14. Every time I go to sleep, the sun goes down. My sleeping must cause the sun to set.

    15. Fallacy (Correlation Implies Causation); The sun would still set even if the person stayed awake all night.

  15. Jason told his friends that they should not swim in the river because there is a sign that says the river is contaminated by mercury.

    16. Reasonable argument
