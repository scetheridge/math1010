3.3b Dimensional Analysis

Use dimensional analysis to make the following conversions:

  1. Menâ€™s jeans in Europe have sizes labeled according to waist measurements in centimeters. What size European jeans should a man with a 34 inch waist buy?

    2. \(\begin{equation} \frac{34 \text { in }}{1} \times \frac{2.54 \text { cm }}{1 \text { in }}=86.4 \text { cm} \end{equation} \)

  2. The circumference of the earth is 40,075 km. How many miles is it around the equator?

    3. \(\begin{equation} \frac{40,075 \text { km}}{1 \text { circumference }} \times \frac{1 \text { mi}}{1.61 \text{ km}}=24,891.3 \text { mi} \end{equation} \)

  3. How many miles is a 5K (5 km) road race?

    4. \(\begin{equation} \frac{5 \text{ km}}{1 \text { race }} \times \frac{1 \text{ mi}}{1.61 \text{ km}}= \text {3.1 mi per race} \end{equation} \)

  4. A roll of dental floss contains 100 yards of floss. If you use 18 inches of floss per day, how many days will the roll last?

    5. \(\begin{equation} \frac{100 \text { yds} }{1 \text { roll }} \times \frac{36 \text { in} }{1 \text { yd} } \times \frac{1 \text { day }}{18 \text { in }}=200 \text { rolls/day} \end{equation} \)

  5. A quarter coin is 1.75 mm thick. How many quarters would it take to make a stack 2 inches high?

    6. \(\begin{equation} \frac{1 \text { quarter }}{1.75 \text{ mm}} \times \frac{25.4 \text{ mm}}{1 \text{ in}} \times \frac{2 \text { in }}{1 \text { stack }}= \text {29 quarters/stack} \end{equation} \)

  6. The speed limit on some highways in France is 110 km/hr. What is this speed limit in miles per hour?

    7. \(\begin{equation} \frac{110 \text{ km}}{1 \text{ hr}} \times \frac{1 \text{ mi}}{1.61 \text{ km}}= \text {68 mph} \end{equation} \)

  7. To walk across the continental United States from east to west, you would probably walk about 3000 miles. If your average stride measures 2.2 feet, how many steps would it take you to walk across the country?

    8. \(\begin{equation} \frac{3000 \text{ mi}}{1 \text {trek}} \times \frac{5280 \text{ ft}}{1 \text{ mi}} \times \frac{1 \text { step }}{2.2 \text{ ft}}=7,200,000 \text{ steps per trek} \end{equation} \)

  8. An iPhone 6 measures 5.44 inches in length, whereas an iPhone 6 Plus measures 6.22 inches. How many more millimeters in length is the iPhone 6 Plus?

    9. \(\begin{equation} \frac{5.44 \text{ in}}{1 \text { iPhone 6}} \times \frac{25.4 \text{ mm}}{1 \text { in }}=138.2 \text { mm per iPhone 6} \end{equation} \)

    \(\begin{equation} \frac{6.22 \text { in }}{1 \text { iPhone 6+}} \times \frac{25.4 \text{ mm}}{1 \text { in }}=158 \text{ mm} \text { per iPhone 6+} \end{equation} \)

    \(\begin{equation} 158-138.2=19.8 \text{ mm difference} \end{equation} \)

  9. You are planning a trip to Venice in December. The average high temperature for that time of year is 7 degrees Celsius. What Fahrenheit temperature is this?

    10. \(\begin{equation} F=\frac{9}{5}(7)+32=44.6^{\circ} \mathrm{F} \end{equation} \)

  10. You are making homemade candy. The recipe says to boil the candy mixture until it reaches 250 degrees Fahrenheit. However, your candy thermometer only shows Celsius degrees. To what temperature should you cook the candy?

    11. \(\begin{equation} C=\frac{5}{9}(250-32)=121.1^{\circ} \mathrm{C} \end{equation} \)

  11. A wooden bridge over a small creek on a mountain road has a 1 ton limit. Your Honda Civic manual says your car weight 1179 kg. Can you safely drive across the bridge? How do you know?

    12. \(\begin{equation} \frac{1 \text { Ton }}{2000 \text{ lbs}} \times \frac{2.2 \text{ lbs}}{1 \text{ kg}} \times \frac{1179 \text{ kg}}{1 \text { Civic }}=1.3 \text{ Tons per Civic} \end{equation} \)

    No, the car is too heavy to be driven over the bridge.

  12. What is the gasoline capacity in liters for a car that holds 13 gallons?

    13. \(\begin{equation} \frac{13 \text{ gal}}{1 \text{ tank}} \times \frac{1 \text{ L}}{0.2642 \text{ gal}}=49 \text{ Liters per tank} \end{equation} \)
