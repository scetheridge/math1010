  2. Your backyard currently has no grass. You want to seed your backyard and enclose it with a fence for your dog.
    1. Determine the perimeter of your backyard.

      2. \(24+12+9+9+15+21=90 ft\)

      3. \(90 \times 14 = $1260\)

  3. You have a rectangular driveway with a circular garden in the middle of it.

    1. Determine the perimeter and area of your rectangular driveway.

      2. \(P = 6+6+9+9 =\) 50 yards

      \(A = 19 * 6 =\) 114 square yards

    2. The circular garden has a radius of 2 yards. Determine the area of the garden.

      3. \( \begin{equation} A=\pi r^{2} \end{equation} \)

      \( \begin{equation} A=\pi \text { * 2}^{2} \end{equation} \)

      The area of the garden is 12.57 square yards.

    3. Your driveway is cracked and needs repaving. Calculate the area of the driveway that would be covered with new asphalt.

      4. The area of the garden is 12.57 square yards

      The area of the entire driveway is \(A=lw=19 \times 6 = 114\; yds^2\)

      \(114-12.57=101.43\)

      The area to be covered by the new asphalt is 101.43 square yards.

    4. If it costs $20.70 to repave each square yard with asphalt, then how much would you pay to repave your driveway?

      5. \(101.43 \times $20.70 = $2099.60\)
